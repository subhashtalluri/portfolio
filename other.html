Advisory Board Member


Subhash Talluri serves on the advisory committee of the Precision Medicine Initiative at the Libin Cardiovascular Institute of the University of Calgary, Canada. Additionally, he also serves as an advisory member for the Advanced Networks Research Lab at the University of Texas at Dallas, reflecting his expertise and contributions to both precision medicine and advanced network research.
  • The Libin Cardiovascular Institute Precision Medicine Initiative aims to unify Alberta’s world-renowned data sources into a comprehensive "super" database, enabling researchers to conduct groundbreaking cardiovascular research. As an Advisory Committee member, Subhash plays a key role in ensuring that the initiative aligns with best practices and leverages advancements in data science, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and other critical areas to build a world-class cardiac data ecosystem.
  • The Advanced Networks Research Lab was established at the University of Texas at Dallas in 1999 with the goal of producing high-quality research in the area of computer and telecommunication networks. Subhash provides technical advice about the direction of research from an industry perspective and contributes technical expertise towards the development and implementation of solutions. Current research is related to network slicing, and the use of AI/ML in network control decisions with the goal of automating resource management and orchestration to support zero-touch network slicing in 5G networks.
