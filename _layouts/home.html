Brief Bio

Help customers design and develop machine learning solutions that leverage the AI/ML stack on AWS via POCs and MVPs, evangelize AWS AI design patterns and best practices for the telecom industry and provides technical subject matter expertise to the business development team in the development of market penetration strategies to enable broad use of AWS within enterprise customers.

Career Aspiration
Data science is transforming every industry at a rapid pace. While there is a huge potential for machine learning, it is equally challenging to fully integrate data science into business. Over a career spanning 10+ years, I had the opportunity to work with customers, system integrators and cloud providers. Most companies trying to achieve digital transformation have often resulted in wasted resources and efforts due to lack of strategic vision. In part, this is because the leadership and higher/middle management are not fully aware of the practicality of data science for improving their business outcomes. There is clearly a lack of data scientists in the decision-making authority to steer the companies in the right direction. I choose to fill that gap using my cross functional expertise at the intersection of engineering, computer science, machine learning and business to provide quality and scalable solutions.
