Brief Bio

I help customers design and develop machine learning solutions that leverage the AI/ML stack on AWS via POCs and MVPs, evangelize AWS AI design patterns and best practices for the telecom industry and provides technical subject matter expertise to the business development team in the development of market penetration strategies to enable broad use of AWS within enterprise customers.

Career Aspiration
Over a career spanning 12+ years, I had the opportunity to work with customers, system integrators and cloud providers. In my experience, integrating data science into business is challenging. Most organizations are unable to move past the 'proof-of-concept' phase and there is clearly a lack of data scientists in the decision-making authority to steer the companies in the right direction. I choose to fill this gap using my cross functional expertise at the intersection of engineering, computer science, machine learning and business to provide quality and scalable solutions.
