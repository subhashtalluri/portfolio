Most organizations are unable to move past the 'proof-of-concept' phase, and there is clearly a lack of data scientists in the decision-making authority to steer companies in the right direction. I choose to fill this gap using my cross-functional expertise at the intersection of engineering, computer science, machine learning, and business to provide quality and scalable solutions.
